Games Guide

🃏 Matatu

Ugandan card game. Match suit or rank. Special cards: 2 (pick up), 8 (skip), Jack (reverse), Ace (change suit), 7 (chop — only matching cut card suit).

♟️ Chess

Classic chess with three AI difficulties: Easy (random), Stockfish (strong), Minimax (very strong). Online multiplayer supported.

🔴 Checkers

8×8 draughts. Diagonal moves, mandatory captures, multi-jumps, king promotion. Online multiplayer supported.

🎲 Ludo

2–4 players. Roll 6 to enter. Land on opponent to send them home. First to get all 4 tokens to center wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play online? ▼
Tap "Play Online" from the home screen, choose which game to challenge with, then tap any available player to send them a challenge. They'll receive a notification and can accept or decline. If they accept, the game starts automatically.
Why can't I see any online players? ▼
Players only appear when they are actively on the Play Online screen. Make sure you and your friends both open the app and navigate to Play Online at the same time. Players who close the app are automatically marked offline.
How does the Party Timer work? ▼
Tap the hourglass icon on the home screen. Choose a preset time (30s to 15min), then press Start. When time runs out your phone vibrates. Rotate your phone to instantly reset the timer — great for passing between players at a party!
What is the 7 rule in Matatu? ▼
At the start of a Matatu game, a random card is placed face-up as the "cut card". Only the 7 that matches the cut card's suit can "chop" (force the next player to pick up cards). The other three 7s are played as normal cards.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your game history, stats, and profile data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
The app is crashing — what should I do? ▼
Try these steps: 1) Force close and reopen the app. 2) Check your internet connection. 3) Update the app to the latest version from the App Store or Play Store. 4) If the problem persists, email us at hello@infoholiks.com with your device model and OS version.
Is Cheza Games free? ▼
Yes! Cheza Games is completely free to download and play, including all online features. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
Which countries is Cheza Games available in? ▼
Cheza Games is available worldwide on Android and iOS. While the games are rooted in Ugandan culture, anyone anywhere can download and enjoy them.
